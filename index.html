About me

After a MSc in machine learning (MVA), I started a PhD in September 2020 under the supervision of Francis Bach and Silv√®re Bonnabel. I work on the interface between machine learning and robotics using variational inference to build new algorithms for online learning, kalman filtering and stochastic control. I also work part-time as a research engineer on smart sensors (infrared camera and radar).

Research

We propose new algorithms to solve nonlinear Bayesian estimation problems based on the variational approximation of an arbitrary distribution with a Gaussian or a mixture of Gaussians.

My research interests include:

  • Variational inference

  • Kalman filtering

  • Dynamic programming

  • Bayesian Machine Learning

  • Robotics
